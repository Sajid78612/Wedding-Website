Dear friends and family,
With hearts full of joy and gratitude, we invite you to share in our happiness as we, Taslima and Shaharia, unite in marriage. This celebration will take place on the 25th of August 2024, an auspicious day we eagerly anticipate.

In the spirit of shared joy and in anticipation of beginning this new chapter under the blessings of Allah, we look forward to celebrating our special day surrounded by our cherished friends and family. Your presence will add to the joy of our wedding day, and we can't wait to share these moments with you.
